# Corporate Structure

## Holding Company

**Fair Software Limited** is the holding company and the entity into which investors invest.

Fair Software Limited is registered in England and Wales.

## Subsidiaries

Fair Software Limited owns 100% of the following entities:

### Equals Collective Limited
- Holds all Equals intellectual property
- The operating entity for the Equals music social network
- Registered in England and Wales

### Fair.xyz Limited
- Shell company for the inactive and wound-down previous NFT business
- No active operations
- Registered in England and Wales

## Structure Diagram

```
Fair Software Limited (Holdco - investor entity)
|
|-- 100% --> Equals Collective Limited (IP holder, operating entity)
|
|-- 100% --> Fair.xyz Limited (shell, inactive)
```
