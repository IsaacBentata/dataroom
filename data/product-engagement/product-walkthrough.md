# Product Walkthrough

Equals is a music social network where every interaction is between verified humans. No bots, no algorithmic slop - just real people connecting through the music they love.

---

## The Feed

The feed is split into "For You" and "Friends" tabs, and everything revolves around music.

Users share hot takes, write album reviews with star ratings, and post about what they're listening to. Every post is tied to a track or artist with inline playback - you can hear what someone's talking about while you read their take. Posts show a music match percentage (e.g. "72%") so you immediately know how similar your taste is to the poster.

This isn't a content feed designed to keep you scrolling. It's a conversation. Users reply, repost, and engage around music they actually care about.

## Meet / Friend Recommendations

Friend recommendations appear inline in the feed as "Suggested for you" cards. Each card shows the person's photo, age, location, bio, a music match percentage (e.g. "67% similar"), and the specific artists you have in common (ROSE, aespa, Central Cee).

One tap to add them as a friend. Music gives people something meaningful to bond over from the first interaction - and 84% of Gen Z say it's their number one conversation starter.

## Artist Profiles

Every artist has a dedicated profile with four tabs:

- **Highlight** - bio, chat rooms with live member counts, music quizzes, supporters, and featured content
- **Reviews** - user reviews with star ratings and attached tracks
- **Related Posts** - all community posts about this artist
- **Music** - full catalogue: top rated, albums, singles/EPs, and tracks with community ratings

The real engagement happens in the chat rooms and review tabs - fans talking to fans about the music they love.

## Album Pages and Digital Vinyls

Tap into any album and you get the full tracklist with individual track ratings, a community rating distribution chart, and user reviews. The "Collect Album" button is where Digital Vinyls come in - branded digital downloads that users pin to their profile.

Labels love this because download sales count toward chart positioning. If they activate through Equals, their artists rocket to the top of the charts. It's a net-new revenue stream that doesn't cannibalise streaming.

## Music Player and Track Details

The music player shows the track, artist, playback controls, and social context - who the first collector was, the top collector, how many total collectors, supporters, and the community rating. You can collect individual tracks as Digital Vinyls directly from the player.

## Music Quizzes

Quizzes test your knowledge of specific artists. You hear a snippet, see the artist, and pick the right song name - timed and competitive. Results are shareable and contribute to your superfan status.

Users come back to prove they know their favourite artists better than anyone else - and the results give them something to talk about with other fans.

## Chats and Chat Rooms

The Chats tab is split into two sections:

**Chat Rooms** - horizontal scroll of artist chat rooms (ROSE, Aespa, Billie Eilish, Drake, IVE) showing live member counts. These are where fans discuss new releases, share hot takes, and connect in real-time. Every chat room is moderated by AI to keep things safe.

**Direct Messages** - personal conversations with friends you've connected with. Each message thread shows the person's favourite artist badge, their streak count, and online status. There's also a "Blast" button for reaching out to new connections.

Before entering any chat, users agree to messaging rules - be respectful, keep it clean, zero tolerance for harassment. This is how we keep 100% of interactions genuine.

## Activity and Viewers

The Activity tab shows who's been viewing your profile - "85 people viewed your profile" with top viewers highlighted (showing how many times they viewed and when). You can add viewers as friends directly.

This tab also shows your friends list and is the gateway to the invite friends bottom sheet and pro subscription.

## User Profiles and Vinyl Grid

Every user profile shows:

- Profile photo, name, bio, location
- Vinyl Grid - a curated display of collected Digital Vinyls pinned as a visual music identity
- Tabs for About, Posts, Reposts, and Collection
- Music quiz results, badges, friends, and activity

The profile is designed to be a music passport - one place that tells someone everything about your taste. The Vinyl Grid is the centrepiece: users arrange their collected vinyls to express who they are through music.

Both private and public profile views are supported, with verification badges for trusted accounts.

## Community Chat Rooms

Artist chat rooms can be configured as public, private (link only), or private with conditions (e.g. must be following the artist). Public rooms with conditions can also require approval before joining.

This flexibility lets artists and community managers create the right environment for their fanbase - from open discussion rooms to exclusive fan clubs.

## Why it all fits together

Every feature is connected by the same thread: music as the foundation for human connection.

The feed gives you opinions. The quizzes test your knowledge. The artist profiles give you depth. The meet cards give you people. The chat rooms and DMs give you community. And the Vinyl Grid gives you identity.

The result is a product where users spend 37 minutes a day - more than Instagram or Snapchat - because they're not consuming content. They're building relationships around something they genuinely care about.
