# Product Roadmap

## Where we are today (April 2026)

500K MAUs, up from 50K six months ago. 34% D7 retention, approaching 20% D30. Users spend 37 minutes a day on the app. Full worldwide catalogue deal with UMG signed, term sheets from Warner and Sony pending.

We have a working product with strong engagement. The next phase is about compounding what's already working.

## 6 months - Q4 2026: Foundation for network effects

**Growth:** 2M MAUs, D7 retention 40%+, D30 retention 30%+. CPI reduced by a further 25% as organic share of acquisition crosses 60%.

**Music industry:** Launch our first major direct-to-fan activation with a top-tier UMG artist, fully capturing their fanbase onto Equals. All three major label partnerships live (UMG signed, Warner and Sony closed). Pre-release exclusives running on the platform - labels using Equals as a direct channel to superfans ahead of streaming.

**Product:** Roll out the AirBuds-equivalent feature (see what friends are listening to in real-time) as a single feature within Equals. Expand Digital Vinyls into a full artist activation toolkit for labels. Launch invite-driven growth loops (k-factor mechanics across multiple surfaces).

## 12 months - Q2 2027: Category definition

**Growth:** 5M MAUs, D7 retention 45%+, D30 retention 35%+. 90% of acquisition organic - Equals becomes a word-of-mouth product.

**Music industry:** 10+ artist activations completed, with Equals established as the default platform for direct-to-fan engagement. Labels actively routing marketing spend through Equals as a fan engagement channel, creating our first meaningful B2B revenue stream.

**Product:** Live event ticketing generating revenue across multiple markets. Social streaming beta - not a Spotify clone, but a fundamentally social listening experience built on the taste graph and friendships we already own. People listen together, react together, discover together.

## 18 months - Q4 2027: The music social network

**Growth:** 15M MAUs, D7 retention 50%+, D30 retention 40%+.

**Product:** Equals is the go-to destination for music culture - where fans hear about new music first, where artists build their most engaged communities, and where labels launch campaigns. Diversified revenue across advertising, ticketing, label partnerships, and premium features. Full social streaming experience live, leveraging our catalogue deals to create experiences Spotify and Apple Music can't replicate.
