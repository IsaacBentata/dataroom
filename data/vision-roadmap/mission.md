# Mission and Vision

## Uniting the world through music

Equals exists to unite the world - and we're doing it through its most universal language: music.

Music crosses every border, language, and generation. It's how people find each other. 84% of Gen Z say music is their number one conversation starter with new people. 60% say being part of a music fandom makes them feel less alone. And yet no platform has ever been built to turn that into real human connection. That's what we're building.

## The last real social network

Every other social platform has gone the same direction: algorithmic content feeds designed to keep you scrolling, filled with bots, AI-generated posts, and synthetic engagement. Go on Instagram, Twitter, TikTok - you don't know what's real and what isn't. Most comments are bots talking to bots.

Equals is different. Every person on our platform is verified. Every conversation is between real humans. We don't even call them users - we call them humans, because that's what they are. No bots, no automated accounts, no algorithmic slop.

This isn't a feature we built on top. It's the foundation. In a world where AI is making the rest of the internet less human, a place where every interaction is genuine becomes more valuable, not less.

## Why the music industry needs this

Streaming revenues have plateaued. Labels built their business on distribution, but distribution is now commoditised - Spotify and Apple Music account for roughly 80% of label revenue, and labels have very little direct relationship with their consumers.

Equals changes that. We're the platform where labels can speak directly to fans, activate artist releases, run pre-release exclusives, and build communities around their artists. UMG has already signed a full worldwide catalogue deal with us - the only superfan/social music platform to do so this year - and they've told us directly they want to use Equals as a core part of their activation strategy.

By building the place where people connect through music, we're also providing the music industry with:

- A new revenue stream through Digital Vinyls, exclusive pre-releases, and direct fan engagement
- Direct consumer relationships that labels have never had access to
- First-party data on fan behaviour and preferences that doesn't exist anywhere else

We're not a music app that happens to be social. We're a social network built on music - and that makes us the distribution layer the music industry has been missing.

## Where this goes

Music is how we got in the door. Connection is the product. The infrastructure we're building - verified identities, taste-based matching, real community - applies to every form of culture. Film, fashion, sport, art. But right now, music is the strongest foundation because it's the one thing that genuinely transcends everything.

At scale, Equals becomes the last true social network - built on the one thing that crosses every language and border.
